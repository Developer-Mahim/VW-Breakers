About VW Breakers

VW Breakers is a family-run business with over 20 years of experience in the Volkswagen parts industry. We specialize in breaking Volkswagen Group vehicles including Volkswagen, Audi, SEAT, and Škoda.

Our team of experts carefully dismantle each vehicle to ensure the highest quality parts are available to our customers. All parts come with a warranty for your peace of mind.
